# Landing page image assets

The landing page (`components/landing/`) looks for the images below in
this directory. Until a file exists, the page renders a styled placeholder in
its spot — drop the finished `.webp` here with the exact filename and it
appears without any code change.

Shared style notes for every prompt: the game's palette is near-black forged
iron (#090a0a–#252827), warm gray stone, muted pewter gold (#9a968d), and
blood red (#8f1e16 / #bd3b2e). Moody torchlit dark fantasy, painterly, no
text, no logos, no watermarks, no UI.

## hero-keyart.webp (~2560×1440, displayed behind the hero at 45% opacity)

> Epic dark fantasy key art, wide cinematic composition: a lone armored
> warrior with a torch stands on a stone bridge before the gates of a
> medieval walled city at dusk, mist rolling in from a dark forest below,
> distant dragon silhouette circling a mountain peak, embers drifting in the
> air. Palette of near-black iron, cold gray stone, and deep blood-red light
> from the torch and sky. Painterly, moody, high detail, no text, no
> watermark. The center will sit behind a logo, so keep the middle third
> relatively calm/dark.

## vocation-*.webp (5 files, portrait ~640×1140, card backgrounds in "Choose Your Path")

Files: `vocation-knight.webp`, `vocation-paladin.webp`, `vocation-sorcerer.webp`,
`vocation-druid.webp`, `vocation-monk.webp`. Each renders behind a pixel-art
character sprite and a text block in the lower third, so keep the lower third
dark and uncluttered. Shared prompt frame:

> Tall portrait dark fantasy painting, moody torchlit atmosphere, palette of
> near-black iron, gray stone, and blood-red light. Atmospheric background
> only — dramatic scenery, no readable text, no logos. Keep the bottom third
> very dark and low-detail so text can sit over it.

Per-vocation subject (append to the frame):

- **knight** — a battle-worn sword and tower shield resting against a scarred
  stone wall in a torchlit castle armory, red banner in shadow behind.
- **paladin** — a longbow and quiver leaning on a mossy standing stone at the
  edge of a misty forest clearing, pale dawn light.
- **sorcerer** — a gnarled staff crackling with faint red arcane fire on a
  stone altar in a dark tower study, floating embers.
- **druid** — a crystal-tipped staff amid glowing ice flowers on a frozen
  forest shrine, cold blue-white moonlight (the one cool-toned card).
- **monk** — worn hand-wraps and a prayer-bead chain on a stone meditation
  plinth in a candlelit mountain sanctum.
